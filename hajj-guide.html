A Complete Guide to Performing Umrah

Learn about the sacred rituals and their significance

Umrah is a pilgrimage to Makkah that can be performed at any time of the year. While it is not obligatory like Hajj, it holds great spiritual significance and rewards. Here is a step-by-step guide to help you perform Umrah correctly.

Step 1: Ihram - Entering the Sacred State

What is Ihram?

Ihram is the sacred state a pilgrim must enter before crossing the Miqat (boundary). For men, it involves wearing two white unstitched cloths. Women wear their normal modest clothing.


Steps to Enter Ihram:

  • Perform Ghusl (ritual bath) or Wudu
  • Men wear the two white cloths; women wear modest clothing
  • Pray two Rakats of Salah
  • Make the intention (Niyyah) for Umrah
  • Recite the Talbiyah: "Labbayk Allahumma Labbayk..."
Step 2: Tawaf - Circling the Kaaba

What is Tawaf?

Tawaf involves walking around the Kaaba seven times in an anti-clockwise direction, starting from the Black Stone (Hajr al-Aswad).


How to Perform Tawaf:

  • Begin at the Black Stone, facing it
  • Make intention for Tawaf
  • If possible, kiss or touch the Black Stone; otherwise, point towards it
  • Walk around the Kaaba seven times
  • Men should perform Raml (walking briskly) in the first three circuits
  • Make Dua throughout the Tawaf
  • After completing, pray two Rakats behind Maqam Ibrahim
Step 3: Sa'i - Walking Between Safa and Marwa

What is Sa'i?

Sa'i commemorates Hajar's search for water for her son Ismail. It involves walking seven times between the hills of Safa and Marwa.


How to Perform Sa'i:

  • Start at Safa hill
  • Face the Kaaba and make Dua
  • Walk towards Marwa (this is one lap)
  • Men should jog between the green lights
  • Upon reaching Marwa, face the Kaaba and make Dua
  • Walk back to Safa (this is the second lap)
  • Complete seven laps, ending at Marwa
Step 4: Halq or Taqsir - Shaving or Trimming Hair

Completing Umrah:

After completing Sa'i, the final step is to cut your hair:


  • Men: Either shave the head completely (Halq) or trim hair equally from all parts (Taqsir). Shaving is preferred.
  • Women: Cut a fingertip's length from the end of their hair

After this, you are released from the state of Ihram and your Umrah is complete. All restrictions of Ihram are now lifted.

Step 5: Duas and Prayers

Important Duas:


Talbiyah:

"Labbayk Allahumma Labbayk. Labbayk La Shareeka Laka Labbayk. Innal Hamda Wan Ni'mata Laka Wal Mulk. La Shareeka Lak."


At the Black Stone:

"Bismillahi Wallahu Akbar"


Between Yemeni Corner and Black Stone:

"Rabbana Atina Fid Dunya Hasanatan Wa Fil Akhirati Hasanatan Wa Qina Azaban Naar"

Tips

Important Tips for Pilgrims

Make your journey smooth with these helpful tips

01

Physical Preparation

Start walking daily weeks before your trip. Umrah involves significant walking, so build your stamina.

02

Learn the Rituals

Study the steps of Umrah beforehand. Attend pre-departure sessions and learn essential Duas.

03

Pack Wisely

Bring comfortable footwear, necessary medications, and modest clothing. Travel light.

04

Stay Hydrated

Drink plenty of Zamzam water. The climate can be hot, so keep yourself hydrated throughout.
