Our Story

Your Trusted Partner for Sacred Journeys

Muqaddas International Tours has been serving pilgrims for over 14 years, helping thousands of Muslims fulfill their dream of visiting the holy cities of Makkah and Madinah.

Founded with a mission to provide hassle-free and spiritually enriching pilgrimage experiences, we have grown to become one of the most trusted names in Hajj and Umrah services in Gujarat.

Our team of experienced professionals and religious scholars ensures that every pilgrim receives personalized attention and guidance throughout their journey. From visa processing to accommodation, transportation to spiritual guidance - we handle everything so you can focus on your ibadah.
